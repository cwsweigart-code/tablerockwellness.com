Our Approach

A higher perspective on your relationship

"Most relationship struggles aren't about not loving each other — they're about not having the right tools."

Just as Table Rock rises above the city and offers a clearer view of the valley below, we help you step back from the day-to-day conflict and see your relationship from a higher vantage point. We work with both couples and individuals — whether you're navigating persistent conflict, trying to rebuild connection, or simply want a stronger foundation for the future.


Our coaching is forward-focused and action-oriented. We don't dwell in the past — we help you understand what's happening now and build the skills to create something better.

The People Behind the Practice

Our team

Every coach at Table Rock Wellness shares the same commitment — helping you and your partner find a clearer, more connected path forward.

Jordan Ellis
Relationship Coach

Jordan specializes in working with individuals navigating the emotional aftermath of relationship breakdown — separation, divorce, and rebuilding after loss. Her approach centers on self-discovery and forward momentum, helping clients understand not just what went wrong, but how to show up differently in the relationships ahead.

Craig Sweigart
Relationship Coach

Craig founded Table Rock Wellness on the belief that most couples in crisis aren't failing for lack of love — they're failing for lack of tools. With a background in communications, philosophy, and psychology, he brings a grounded, empathetic approach to helping couples and individuals break the patterns keeping them stuck.

Avery Morgan
Relationship Coach

Avery works primarily with couples who are committed to staying together but stuck in repeating cycles they can't seem to break. Her approach is structured and practical — she helps couples develop concrete communication tools, identify their specific conflict patterns, and build new habits that hold up outside the coaching room.

How It Works

Four steps to a clearer path

Getting started is straightforward. No long waitlists, no complicated intake process — just a simple path to real support.

01
Free Consultation

A 20-minute call to understand your situation and confirm we're the right fit for you. No pressure, no obligation.

02
Intake & Goals

We learn about your relationship history, your current challenges, and what success looks like for you.

03
Coaching Sessions

Regular sessions — weekly or bi-weekly — focused on practical tools, honest conversation, and real progress.

04

Track your progress, build new habits, and continue as long as it's useful — or wrap up when you've reached your goals.
