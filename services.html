Common Questions

What people ask before booking

Does my partner have to come to every session?

For couples packages, both partners attend together. However, individual sessions are available for anyone who wants to work on their own patterns regardless of whether their partner participates. Sometimes one person doing the work is enough to shift the dynamic.

Is coaching the same as therapy?

No. Coaching is forward-focused and goal-oriented — we work on where you're going, not processing past trauma or treating mental health conditions. Craig is not a licensed therapist or counselor. If clinical treatment is what you need, he'll tell you honestly and help point you in the right direction.

What if we're not sure we want to stay together?

That's okay. The goal of coaching isn't to guarantee you stay together — it's to help you both show up with more clarity and intention, whatever you decide. Some clients find their way back to each other. Others find a clearer, more respectful path forward apart.

How quickly can we start?

After your free Clarity Call, sessions can typically be scheduled within 1–2 weeks. If your situation feels urgent, mention that on your call and Craig will do his best to accommodate.

What's your cancellation policy?

24 hours notice is required to cancel or reschedule without charge. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged the full session rate.

Not sure where to start?

The Clarity Call is free, takes 20 minutes, and gives you a clear sense of whether coaching is right for your situation.
